About me

My name is Fallon Pulido. Currently, I am a student of interactive media design, where I have had the opportunity to learn various subjects that I have enjoyed quite a bit and that will be useful for me in pursuing what I want to do.

I was born in Colombia, studied a degree in human resources, and began my career as a psychologist. I made the decision to migrate, and in 2017, I moved to Australia. I had the chance to live there for about 5 years, during which I learned the language, traveled, explored many beautiful beaches, got married, and became a mother.

After a while and due to various circumstances, my family and I decided to change countries and began the process to move to Canada. Starting practically from scratch has been challenging but rewarding as we see the results.
